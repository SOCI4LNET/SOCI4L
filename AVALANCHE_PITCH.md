## 🎤 Presentation Script

**Hi, I’m Meriç.**  
I’ll start with a quick background, then introduce **SOCI4L**.  
I’m a creative director and founder of Floyka, a design and product studio.  
I’m also a Team1 Türkiye member, and I began building SOCI4L through **Avalanche Build Games**.

Today in Web3, wallets hold assets, history, and reputation — but they’re not human-readable or socially usable. We still share raw, complex addresses instead of real identities.

**SOCI4L changes this. We turn any EVM wallet into a customizable, measurable, and shareable public profile.**

You can create a SOCI4L profile with any EVM address, but crucial profile actions — like claims and username registrations (what we call *slugs*) — are recorded right here on the **Avalanche C-Chain**.

Instead of a "0x…" address, you get a premium public identity surface. A profile with your links, automated portfolio value, NFT showcases, social context, and multi-chain activity — all tied safely to your wallet without giving up custody.

This makes SOCI4L an **Avalanche-native public profile system** built for the broader EVM ecosystem.

### What sets us apart right now?
1. **Discoverability & Composability:** Profiles are wallet-claimed, and slugs are registered on-chain. This creates portable public endpoints that any app can resolve. 
2. **On-chain Growth:** Every slug claim and profile action generates Avalanche transactions.
3. **Direct On-Chain Support (The Donate Feature):** We’ve recently integrated an on-chain **Donate/Tipping** smart contract. Anyone can directly support a creator or builder via their SOCI4L profile using AVAX, settled instantly on-chain with our custom infrastructure.

So SOCI4L is **not** just another link-in-bio tool. It’s a universal public profile and identity layer for wallets — anchored firmly on Avalanche.

### What’s Next? (Our Future Vision)
We already have working profiles, wallet-based claiming, real-time analytics, and live public pages. But we are moving faster:
- **Developer Resolution Layer (API):** Opening our registry so any third-party dApp or DAO can use SOCI4L as their base identity layer.
- **Ecosystem Integrations:** Deep integration with tools like **Luma** to show Web3 event attendance and hosting directly on profiles.
- **On-chain Social Verification:** Cryptographically proving ownership of Web2 accounts like X or GitHub on the blockchain.
- **Multi-Chain Expansion:** Scaling our footprint while keeping Avalanche as our home base.

Our vision is simple:  
**Every wallet becomes a public identity.**

SOCI4L makes Web3 users visible, expressive, connected, and supported.

Thank you.
